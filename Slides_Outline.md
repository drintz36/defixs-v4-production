# DeFixs — Presentation Outline

---

## Introduction

- DeFixs is a website that reads your broken code and tells you what went wrong
- It talks to you like a helpful teacher — simple, clear, and easy to follow
- No sign up or setup needed, just open the site and paste your code

---

## Identifications

- Beginners often see an error message but have no idea what it actually means
- Most AI tools fix the code but never take time to explain why it broke
- AI can also make up fake errors or accidentally break the code format

---

## Set Goals

- Help users truly understand their bugs, not just copy and paste a fix
- Support many coding languages and give answers as fast as possible
- Keep it simple to use — no account, no database, just open and go

---

## Expected Results

- Users get a clear, friendly explanation of what went wrong and how to fix it
- The result is shown in a clean layout that is easy to read at a glance
- The tool stays online by automatically switching to a backup AI when needed

---

## Learning Process

- We gave the AI a clear role — act like a senior developer who loves to teach
- We set strict rules so it always uses simple words and never skips explanations
- We tested and improved the instructions many times until it felt natural

---

## Performance Improvement strategies and Optimization Process and Efforts

- If the main AI is slow, the tool silently switches to a second AI as backup
- A background filter removes any wrong or made-up answers before they appear
- Past sessions are saved in the browser so users can go back and review them
